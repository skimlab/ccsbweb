CCSB Biweekly Seminar Series

CCSB Biweekly Seminar Series is to promote interdisciplinary research across PVAMU campus and beyond, hosted by CRI Center for Computational Systems Biology. We alternate between CCSB researchers and external speakers. We meet in CCSB Conference Room (ELEN 231). We meet virtually over Zoom to mitigate risk associated with COVID-19. Dr. Seungchan Kim is the organizer for the seminar series. Please contact him if you have any suggestion, including speakers.

Note: For Spring 2022, we will continue to use Zoom for the seminars for safety and to comply with PVAMU and CDC guidelines.

Day | Time: Wednesdays | 12pm - 1:00pm
